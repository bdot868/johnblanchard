Our Featured Services

Ground Transportation Management
  • Planning and scheduling routes specifically designed for your event.
  • Managing and supervising bus drivers and on-site coordination staff.
  • Private Event and Exhibitor Transportation Management
  • Plan, coordinate and manage all special transportation needs for events.
  • Airport Meet & Greet
  • City wide tours and spouse programs
Parking Control & Management

Work closely with local municipalities and parking lots to design and manage parking lots fo cars, buses, limousines, VIP parking and drop off. Coordinate a drop-off/pick up zone and plan for a taxi and other commercial vehicles. Design and provide special event parking passes.

Traffic Control & Traffic Management

Work closely with local municipalities to develop and design a traffic pattern tailored for your event. Augment local police or provide traffic control personnel to direct event traffic. Obtain all necessary city, county and state permits for lane closures, no parking zones, or other special street occupancy permitting needs, as required.

Shuttle Bus Services

Provide and manage shuttle buses and/or other ground transportation programs for conventions, trade shows, sporting and special events, corporate meetings and training seminars.

Catered Boxed Meals and/or Snacks

  • Provide on board catered boxed meals, snacks and drinks for guests when time is a factor or a higher level of service is expected or required.
